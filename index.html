About YAKSHA

YAKSHA is a mission-driven rescue initiative focused on rapid, responsible, and reliable response during emergencies. We exist to act when every second matters.

The Rover. Your First Responder.

Built for smoke, rubble, and uncertainty. YAKSHA’s rover platform combines AI, multi-sensor perception, and all-terrain mobility to find life faster—without risking more lives.

  • Multi-spectral sensing: thermal, gas, lidar, and video.
  • Secure live telemetry and operator assist HUD.
  • Designed for harsh, low-visibility environments.
