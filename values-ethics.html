THE YAKSHA DIRECTIVES

Hard-coded Moral Compass

DIRECTIVE 01

LIFE FIRST, ALWAYS

YAKSHA is strictly humanitarian and non-combat. Every decision prioritizes the preservation of human life above all operational objectives.

if (human_life_detected) { prioritize_rescue(); }

DIRECTIVE 02

ALGORITHMIC HONESTY

Our AI is "Explainable", we know why the rover made a decision. Transparency in the dark ensures accountability.

decision_log.append(reason, confidence_score, alternatives)

DIRECTIVE 03

DATA SOVEREIGNTY

Your crisis, your data. Mission data is encrypted and never sold. Partners retain full control over their information.

encrypt(mission_data, partner_key); never_sell(data);

DIRECTIVE 04

ENVIRONMENTAL STEWARDSHIP

Leave no trace. Our rovers are built to be recovered. We don't leave "tech-trash" in nature.

recovery_beacon.enable(); track_all_components();

THE CODE OF CONDUCT

DIRECTIVE 01: HUMAN OVERSIGHT

YAKSHA systems shall never have the final say in life-critical decisions. We provide the data; the rescuer provides the command.

DIRECTIVE 02: TOTAL RELIABILITY

A failure in the field is a failure of our promise. We over-engineer every joint, bolt, and line of code because 'good enough' doesn't save lives.

THE HUMAN-IN-THE-LOOP

The machine is the tool, the human is the intent.
